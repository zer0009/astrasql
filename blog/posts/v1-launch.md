## What is AstraSQL Agent?

AstraSQL Agent is an AI-powered tool that converts natural language questions into SQL queries. But unlike other tools, we've built it with **privacy-first principles** - the AI only sees your database schema (table and column names), never your actual data.

## Why We Built AstraSQL

### The Problem

We've seen too many teams struggle with data access:
- **Non-technical team members** can't query databases
- **SQL knowledge** is a barrier to insights
- **Privacy concerns** prevent using AI-powered tools
- **Complex queries** take too long to write

### Our Solution

AstraSQL makes data accessible to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. Whether you're a product manager exploring metrics, a sales team analyzing performance, or an executive making data-driven decisions, AstraSQL empowers you to get answers instantly.

## Privacy-First Architecture

What sets AstraSQL apart is our **metadata-only AI processing**:

**Traditional AI Tools:**
- Send your entire database to AI providers
- Expose sensitive customer data
- Create compliance risks

**AstraSQL:**
- Sends only schema information (table/column names)
- Your actual data never leaves your database
- GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2 compliant
